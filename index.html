METHOD

METHOD

Our athletic approach to Pilates is designed to strengthen the body through its full range of motion and across all planes of movement, creating a balanced body that moves efficiently, feels stronger, and performs better in everyday life.

We emphasize deep core activation, stability, balance, breathwork, and intentional muscle engagement, creating movement patterns that improve body awareness, coordination, and overall function. Each class is thoughtfully programmed to challenge the body in a safe, intelligent way while maintaining proper alignment and control.

We intentionally keep our classes intimate to create a personalized experience where form, alignment, and individualized attention remain at the forefront. This hands-on approach allows clients to move safely, progress confidently, and get the most from every session.

FOUNDER

FOUNDER

My journey with reformer Pilates began in California, where I quickly fell in love with the method's dynamic and creative nature. Inspired by its endless variations and the ability to do so much on a single apparatus, I pursued my certification and became a Pilates teacher.

With a background as a competitive figure skater, I developed an early appreciation for movement, discipline, and body awareness. My passion for fitness evolved into a love for refined, intentional movement-ultimately leading me to Pilates and its addictive practice. I truly love what I do and haven't found anything quite like it!

Founder and instructor Alexis has been passionate about movement since childhood as a competitive figure skater. Her love of fitness and body awareness eventually led her to Pilates, where she found the perfect balance of strength, control, and challenge.

Her classes are known for delivering a serious workout through smart, intentional movement-helping clients build strength, find their edge, and move with confidence.

Elizabeth Photo

Elizabeth Szatkowski

Pilates Instructor

Elizabeth is a former competitive gymnast whose lifelong relationship with movement began at a young age. Years of high-level training left her with severe back trauma and chronic pain that followed her for more than 15 years. After trying countless forms of exercise, Pilates was the only method that truly healed her body. That experience inspired Elizabeth to become a Pilates teacher. She brings empathy, precision, and athletic intelligence to every session, creating classes that are challenging yet deeply supportive. Her goal is to help clients move out of pain, build strength, and reconnect with their bodies in a sustainable way.

Teresa Photo

Teresa

Barre, Pilates, Sculpt, and Personal Training Instructor

Teresa is a seasoned fitness professional with over 15 years of experience in the health and wellness industry. A former NYPD detective turned fitness enthusiast, she brings strength, discipline, and passion into every class she teaches.

Certified in Barre and experienced across multiple training modalities including Pilates, Sculpt, and personal training, Teresa is known for her dynamic programming style and creative approach to movement. Her classes are thoughtfully designed to challenge the body with deep, effective burns while keeping clients engaged and motivated.
